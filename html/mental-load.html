Mental Load

Understanding Mental Load

Mental load refers to the cognitive burden of managing tasks, responsibilities, and mental processes. It includes both the effort required to remember tasks and the emotional weight of responsibility.

In today's fast-paced world, mental load has increased significantly due to constant connectivity, multitasking, and information overload. This can lead to fatigue, reduced decision-making capacity, and mental exhaustion.

Discover strategies to reduce cognitive overwhelm, prioritize effectively, delegate tasks, and restore mental clarity and focus.
