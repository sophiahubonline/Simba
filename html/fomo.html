FOMO

Understanding FOMO

FOMO, or Fear of Missing Out, is the anxiety that arises from the feeling that others are experiencing more exciting events or opportunities than you are.

In the age of social media, FOMO can be intensified by constant exposure to curated versions of others' lives, leading to feelings of inadequacy and restlessness.

Building contentment with your own life, limiting social media consumption, and practicing gratitude can help you overcome FOMO and find peace in the present moment.
