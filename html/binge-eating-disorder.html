Understanding Binge Eating Disorder

Binge Eating Disorder (BED) is characterized by recurring episodes of eating large quantities of food, often rapidly and without feeling in control.

Unlike other eating disorders, BED is not typically followed by purging behaviors, but often involves feelings of guilt, shame, and distress.

Recovery involves understanding the emotional triggers, developing healthy coping mechanisms, and seeking professional support to build a healthier relationship with food.
