Social Anxiety

Understanding Social Anxiety

Social anxiety is an intense fear of social situations and interactions with others. It involves worry about being judged, embarrassed, or rejected.

People with social anxiety may avoid social gatherings, speaking in public, or even casual conversations, which can significantly impact their quality of life.

With exposure therapy, cognitive techniques, and support, you can gradually overcome social anxiety and build confidence in your interactions with others.
