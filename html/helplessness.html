Helplessness

Understanding Helplessness

Learned helplessness is a state where a person feels unable to control or change their circumstances, often developing after repeated experiences of failure or trauma.

This sense of helplessness can lead to depression, anxiety, and a diminished sense of agency. It becomes a limiting belief that prevents you from taking action to improve your situation.

Through cognitive and behavioral strategies, you can regain your sense of agency, challenge limiting beliefs, and reclaim control over your life.
