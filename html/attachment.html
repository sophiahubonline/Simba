Understanding Attachment

Attachment refers to the emotional bonds we form with others, beginning in early childhood and continuing throughout our lives.

Secure attachment promotes healthy relationships and emotional well-being, while insecure attachment patterns can lead to challenges in relationships and self-perception.

Understanding your attachment style can help you build healthier, more fulfilling relationships and develop greater self-awareness.
