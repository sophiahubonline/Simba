About Us

Our Mission

The SIMBA PROJECT is dedicated to supporting mental health and personal growth through education, community, and evidence-based resources. We believe that everyone's struggle is a call to adventure—a opportunity for transformation and growth.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health is prioritized, stigma is eliminated, and everyone has access to the tools and support they need to embark on their hero's journey.

The Hero's Journey

Inspired by Joseph Campbell's monomyth, we view mental health challenges as part of a larger narrative of growth and transformation. Just like the hero in every great story, we all face challenges that call us to grow, adapt, and discover our inner strength.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach mental health with empathy and understanding.
  • Education: We provide evidence-based information to empower our community.
  • Community: We foster a supportive environment where everyone belongs.
  • Growth: We believe in the potential for continuous personal development.
