Procrastination

Understanding Procrastination

Procrastination is the act of delaying or postponing tasks, often despite knowing it might have negative consequences. It's not laziness, but typically stems from anxiety, perfectionism, or difficulty with task initiation.

Chronic procrastination can lead to stress, decreased productivity, and diminished self-confidence. Understanding the root causes of your procrastination is the first step toward change.

Discover effective strategies to overcome procrastination, build better habits, and develop the motivation to tackle tasks with confidence.
