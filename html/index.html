What is SIMBA?

The site helps you explore mental health topics in a simple way.

SIMBA is built around short tests and clear pages for each notion. You can browse a theme, take a 10-question quiz, read your result, and save your scores in your profile if you log in.

The goal is to make each topic easy to understand and easy to revisit, so you can move from curiosity to self-observation without getting lost.
