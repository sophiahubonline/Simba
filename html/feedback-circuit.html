The Feedback Circuit

Understanding The Feedback Circuit

The Feedback Circuit refers to the continuous cycle of giving and receiving feedback, which is essential for personal and professional growth.

Constructive feedback helps us understand our impact on others, identify blind spots, and develop greater self-awareness. Learning to both give and receive feedback with openness and compassion is a key skill.

Master the art of feedback through understanding different feedback models, practicing empathetic communication, and creating a culture of continuous improvement.
